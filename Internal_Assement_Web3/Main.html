About

My Journey Through Nature

My name is Anjali Kumar, a nature enthusiast and web developer. With a degree in Electronic Communication from Gautam Buddha University (2022), I’ve always been fascinated by the way technology connects the world, much like how plants connect to the ecosystem. As an engineer, I ventured into web development, seeking to merge creativity with technology, much like how nature blends harmony with growth.
To expand my skills, I delved into full-stack web development. Learning technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and databases like MongoDB was as enriching as observing the growth of a tree from seed to bloom. The journey allowed me to build functional web applications, nurturing ideas into vibrant digital landscapes.
One of my proudest achievements was creating a nature-themed website for my Bootstrap project on Bombay Sea Face. This project reflects my passion for nature while showcasing my technical ability to create user-centered and aesthetically pleasing websites.
